Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

The Fort Bookshop (operated by Rising Action Productions LLC) is an independent affiliate bookshop based in Fort Wayne, Indiana. We are committed to being transparent about how this site works and how your information is handled.

Affiliate Disclosure

The Fort Bookshop is an affiliate of Bookshop.org. When you click a link on this site or our social media accounts and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. Every purchase through our links supports both independent booksellers nationwide and this shop.

Information We Collect

This website does not directly collect, store, or process any personal information. We do not have account registration, contact forms, or payment processing on this site.

Third-Party Services

When you click through to Bookshop.org to make a purchase, you are subject to Bookshop.org's own privacy policy and terms of service. We encourage you to review their policies at bookshop.org.

Our social media presence on Instagram, Bluesky, and TikTok is subject to the respective privacy policies of those platforms.

Cookies

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may use cookies as part of standard GitHub services. We do not use cookies to track individual visitors or collect personal data for our own purposes.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our social media channels or via Rising Action Productions LLC, Fort Wayne, Indiana.
